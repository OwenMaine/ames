About Ames Textile

We are a Virginia-based textile agency dedicated to sustainable and ethical clothing manufacturing.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for quality and sustainability.

Ames Textile Inc. was founded in 2007 to fill a crucial gap in the market: providing reliable, high-quality, and sustainable clothing production within the United States. Our founder, drawing from decades of experience in the textile industry, saw a need for a manufacturing partner that brands could truly trust.

Today, we have grown into a leading agency, known for our extensive market know-how and a robust network of manufacturers. We are committed to helping both emerging and established fashion brands bring their visions to life, ethically and sustainably.
