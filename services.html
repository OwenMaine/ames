Our Services

End-to-end solutions for your fashion brand, from initial concept to final production.

Fashion Design & Tech Pack

Our creative team can help you translate your ideas into professional fashion designs and detailed technical sketches. We create comprehensive tech packs that serve as the blueprint for manufacturing, ensuring accuracy and consistency.

  • Mood boards and concept development.
  • Detailed sketches and illustrations.
  • Complete tech packs with measurements and material specs.

Material Sourcing

Finding the right materials is crucial. Our dedicated sourcing team has an extensive network of suppliers, allowing us to find high-quality and sustainable fabrics, trims, and accessories that match your brand's aesthetic and values.

  • Access to a wide range of conventional and sustainable materials.
  • Supplier vetting and quality assurance.
  • Sample procurement and testing.

Sampling & Prototyping

Before full production, we create prototypes and samples for your approval. This critical step allows you to test the fit, feel, and overall quality of your design, ensuring the final product meets your exact specifications.

  • First-fit prototypes to sales samples.
  • Iterative refinement process based on your feedback.
  • Pre-production samples for final approval.

Production Management

We oversee the entire production process, from cutting and sewing to finishing and packaging. Our team works closely with our network of factories to ensure high-quality output, ethical labor practices, and on-time delivery.

  • Coordination with trusted US-based factories.
  • Rigorous quality control at every stage.
  • Logistics and delivery management.
