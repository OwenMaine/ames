Fashion for a Better Future

We are committed to reducing our environmental impact through sustainable materials and ethical practices.

Sustainable Materials

Develop your clothing collection with sustainable materials that reduce environmental impact while maintaining quality and style.

Biodegradable Polyester

An innovative alternative to conventional polyester that maintains performance properties while being designed to break down at the end of its lifecycle, reducing long-term plastic pollution.

Organic Cotton

Grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, organic cotton promotes healthier ecosystems and uses significantly less water than conventional cotton, making it a cornerstone of sustainable fashion.

Recycled Fabrics

We utilize fabrics made from recycled materials, such as rPET (from plastic bottles) and reclaimed textiles. This process diverts waste from landfills and reduces the need for virgin resources.

Our Commitment

More than just materials.

Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond the fabrics we use. We are dedicated to implementing eco-friendly practices throughout our entire supply chain. This includes minimizing waste, reducing water consumption, and ensuring fair and ethical labor conditions in all our partner factories.

By choosing Ames Textile, you are partnering with a manufacturer that cares deeply about the planet and its people.
